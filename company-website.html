Company Website

Project Overview

The Company Website project represents a professional corporate website developed for an IT services company to showcase its services, projects, and contact details in a clean and user-friendly manner.

Key Features

  • Professional corporate UI design
  • Multi-page website structure
  • Responsive layout for all devices
  • Services and projects showcase
  • Integrated AI chatbot for customer support

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Business Objective

The main objective of this project is to establish a strong online presence for the IT company, improve brand visibility, and allow potential clients to easily explore services and make inquiries.

Impact & Benefits

This website helps the company attract new clients, present its expertise professionally, and provide quick access to information through an interactive and modern interface.
